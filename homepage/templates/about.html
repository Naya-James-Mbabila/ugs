| Understanding Gift Shop|


The Understanding Gift Shop Management App is a user-friendly application designed to streamline
the shop management. With its organization and calculation features, it makes it easier to track
sales and monitor available items in stock.

Site Functionality:
The app provides features for stock management, sales management, and a dashboard for an
overview of available stock levels and recent sales transactions.

Stock Management

1. Add new stocks - Limited to admins
2. View existing Stocks
3. Edit stock information - Limited to admins

Sales Management

1. Enter new sales transactions
2. generate receipts for each sale
3. Save receipts to the records folder on the desktop using the print option
